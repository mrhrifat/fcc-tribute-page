Prophet. Mohammad ﷺ

The man who was the latest messenger from Allah

Muhammad ﷺ was an Prophet, best known as the central figure (& last prophet) of Islam religion and is amongst the most revered & important historical figures in the world.

Here's a time line of Prophet. Muhammad ﷺ life:

  • 569 -Death of his father, Abdullah
  • 570- Possible date of birth: 12 or 17 Rabi al Awal: in Mecca Arabia
  • 577 Death of his mother, Amina
  • 595 Meets and marries Khadijah
  • 597 Birth of Zainab, his first daughter, followed by: Ruqayyah, Umm Kulthum, and Fatima Zahra
  • 610 At age 40, Angel Jebreel (Gabriel) was said to appear to Muhammad ﷺ on the mountain and call him "the Prophet of Allah"
  • 610 Begins in secret to gather followers in Mecca
  • 613 Begins spreading message of Islam publicly to all Meccans
  • 614 Heavy persecution of Muslims begins
  • 619 The year of sorrows: Khadija (his wife) and Abu Talib (his uncle) die
  • 620 Isra and Mi'raj (reported ascension to heaven to meet God)
  • 623 Battle of Badr
  • 625 Battle of Uhud
  • 627 Battle of the Trench (also known as the siege of Medina)
  • 629 Conquest of Mecca
  • 632 Farewell pilgrimage, event of Ghadir Khumm, and death, in what is now Saudi Arabia

The best of you is the one who learned and taught the Quran. (Bukhari)
